## Renoir

- 2 oz. London Dry
- 1 oz. Cocchi Americano
- 0.25 oz. Peaty Scotch (Ardbeg 10)
- 2 dashes Feegan's Orange Bitters
- Absinthe rinse (preferably St. George)
- Garnish: Lemon peel or Orange Peel (flamed if orange)
